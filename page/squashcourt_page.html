Booking

Each booking is for 1 hour per person and is limited to 1 court per person. Court bookings are available from 8 am to 8 pm.

Rules & Regulations

  • Proper attire, including non-marking shoes, is mandatory on the squash courts.
  • Players must adhere to the code of conduct, respecting opponents and referees.
  • Scoring follows the point-a-rally system, where a point is scored regardless of the server.
  • A squash game is typically played to 11 points, and players must win by two clear points.
  • Any physical contact or obstructive behavior is strictly prohibited.
